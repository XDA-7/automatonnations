leaders control systems
they have a maximum number of controllable systems
they provide development bonuses to controlled systems
they will also take a fraction of the military produced by their controlled systems
leaders have their own military capacity independent of their empire, also limited by system development
in the event of war, leaders are obliged to provide a fraction of their military to the empire fleet
a record of the military lease is made, losses are distributed equally among leaders
systems won in war will be distributed to leaders with a probability proportional to their military contribution
leaders have a chance of breaking away from their nation
